# Retail Customer Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes 3,900 retail transactions to uncover customer purchasing behavior, revenue drivers, customer segments, and subscription trends.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow:

- Data Cleaning & Preparation using Python
- Business Analysis using PostgreSQL and SQL
- Interactive Dashboard Development in Tableau
- Business Recommendations based on analytical findings

## Business Problem

A retail company wanted to better understand customer purchasing behavior to improve:

- Revenue Growth
- Customer Retention
- Subscription Adoption
- Marketing Effectiveness
- Product Strategy

This project answers the question:

> How can customer shopping data be leveraged to identify trends, improve engagement, and optimize business decisions?

## Key Insights

### Customer Segmentation

- Loyal customers represent the largest customer segment.
- Returning customers present a strong upsell opportunity.

### Revenue Drivers

- Clothing generates the highest revenue.
- Young Adults contribute the highest overall revenue.

### Customer Behavior

- Express shipping customers spend more per transaction.
- Subscription status significantly influences purchasing patterns.

### Product Performance

- Several products consistently achieve high customer ratings.
- Certain products show strong dependence on discount promotions.
